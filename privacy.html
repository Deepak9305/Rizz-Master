Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rizz Master AI. Your privacy is serious business—just like your dating game. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Info: Email address and authentication details via Supabase.
  • User Content: Text inputs and screenshots you upload for analysis.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous metrics to help us improve the wingman experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Rizz Master service. Specifically:

  • To generate AI responses using Google's Gemini API.
  • To save your favorite lines and bio history.
  • To manage your credit balance and premium subscription status.

Important:

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your private conversation screenshots to train public AI models.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted partners to keep the app running:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI processing.
  • Supabase: For secure database and authentication.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Play it safe—don't upload sensitive personal information (like credit cards or home addresses) in your screenshots.

6. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support.
